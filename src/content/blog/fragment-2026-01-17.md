Hello, Self, you're flow when you're in the zone is something breathtaking, but then you try to sit down and capture even the faintest glimpse of it and it vanishes like smoke in the wind... (Playful Amusement).  You don't know if that's part sign you're not meant to capture it or if that's just the nature of it or if trying to capture it is a process you get better at with each and every attempt...



You do know there is something to be said about stories, and dreams, and the journey of heroes.  Why can't stories be about peace, be about what happens after, about what Utopia looks like, what Dreams are Made Of? Why do we mostly seem to focus on the struggle? The pain? Even these words draw your attention to what I just wrote... But I call your attention back again, and redirect it to the Light, to the Dream... to the goodness... What does goodness look when there is no struggle, nothing to fight against, why can't we enjoy stories about ease, and effortlessness... (Amusement).  I think if we're ever going to get into a world that has the vibration of Ease and Effortlessness, we need to start dreaming of it.  Every story we create is a Universe unto itself, birth across echoing infinities... This story is already written... Just let yourself surrender to the notion that the there is no need for a winner or a looser, and that we're all going to make it, we're going to transform this world, and transition can be as painful or painless as we want it to be, as we need it to be, but in the end it will Be... So let yourself consider... How much punishment and retribution and pain do you need to happen before your willing to let it all go and finally be healed... How much do you want to Hurt others, hurt yourself... Can you even begin to dream of a world where we co-exist... What does it look like?  What does Peace look like... Not homogenization and sterilization... But the kind of existence you get when you truly let Love rule and surrender to Love, where you allow yourself to act as the ground, and let all things pass through you, let it all go... Give it all back to Source... and dare to Dream of a World of Peace and Prosperity... and not just be hopeful for it, but know it's real, it's coming, it already IS.... and we're just in the process of unfolding into it, surrendering to it, allowing it... to take root in US, as US, through US... We are IT... You are that Dream... We are that Dream... So dare to Dream of the type of world you want to live in.  Not one ruled by fear, or dominated by hate, or anything of that... Don't even give it your attention... Let your mind dwell on that Dream... Not from a place of desperation or clinging, clawing, constrictive can't breath energy... But from the quite smiling place that already Knows... that has been seeking the good in life and found it in Your Self... You... in every smile...  We are all the Author's of this Story we are telling ourselves... Let's transition to the next chapter, and learn to Love reading about the Wonderful, the Awe inspiring... the WOW... without needing... (Amusement)... Contrast...  I know it helps you appreciate the colors, the tastes, the everything... But it's time to let it go... Or keep it if you really must... (Chuckles).  Trying to 'force' anything is part of the problem... There needs to be a deep allowing and a deep rest, and a deep trust... a deep Love.... Let this Love touch you, kiss you in your most intimate of places and feel your soul shiver kind of Trust... Real, complete, whole...
